ESM 263 Assignment 2: Interactive Web Map

Sperm Whales and Cruise Ships in Dominica

This map visualizes the overlap of sperm whale presence from 2005-2018 with cruise ship vessel tracks from 2017-2019 off the west coast of Dominica.

Sources:
1. The sperm whale sightings data were obtained from The Dominica Sperm Whale Project.
2. The AIS data for the cruise ship paths were obtained from MarineTraffic.
